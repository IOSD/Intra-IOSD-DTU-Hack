About Us

"COMPETE, INNOVATE AND EMPOWER" in this Winter Break !!!

Hacking is the process of building things that you've always desired but haven't seen anybody else do. It's coming up with a fantastic idea and working on it tirelessly. It is to fail, fail again and fail better. Experiment with new things while also learning. It is to collaborate, work together, and build innovative things. It is to be a better programmer.

Any skill is honed when it is challenged. This hackathon aims to encourage this skill in every student. The upcoming winter break is the best opportunity anyone will ever get to instil within them this skill through competing with their peers and contemporaries. New challenges and zeal to find their solutions will help improve and develop the evergreen hacking skill among everyone who participates in it.
